Who we are

WALIDOS LLC is a U.S.-registered digital marketing agency based in Richmond, Kentucky. We specialize in SEO, paid advertising, branding, and digital growth strategies for businesses of all sizes.

Legal entity: WALIDOS LLC

Data we collect

  • Contact details you submit (name, email, phone, company, message) via forms or email.
  • Business information needed to scope or deliver services (website, goals, budgets, project files).
  • Usage data from our site (pages viewed, device, general location) collected via cookies/analytics.
  • Payment/Invoice records for contracted services (handled via our payment providers; we do not store full card details).

How we use your data

  • Responding to inquiries and providing quotes.
  • Preparing, performing, and managing service agreements.
  • Improving our website, services, and customer experience.
  • Sending service-related communications (no spam; you can opt out of non-essential messages).
  • Meeting legal, tax, and accounting obligations.

Legal bases

Where applicable, we rely on one or more of the following bases: performance of a contract; legitimate interests (e.g., operating and improving our services); consent (for optional communications/analytics); and compliance with legal obligations.

Sharing & processors

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with vetted service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, CRM, payment processors) strictly to operate our business and only under appropriate safeguards and contracts.

Cookies & analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies to measure site performance and improve content. You can control cookies in your browser settings. If we use Google Analytics or similar, we configure it to collect aggregated usage data. No sensitive personal data is collected via cookies.

Tip: to limit analytics cookies, set your browser to block third-party cookies or use “Do Not Track.”

Data retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, and to meet legal/accounting requirements. When no longer needed, we delete or anonymize the data.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we continually review our safeguards.

Your privacy rights

Subject to your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your data, and to object to certain processing. To make a request, email contact@walidosllc.store. We will verify and respond within a reasonable time.

International transfers

Our service providers may process data in the U.S. and other countries. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers.

Children’s privacy

Our website and services are intended for business users and are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will reflect the most recent changes.
