About WALIDOS LLC

A U.S.-registered marketing agency dedicated to helping businesses grow with strategy, transparency, and measurable results.

Meet the Founder

WALIDOS LLC was founded and is managed by Walid Anas, based in Richmond, Kentucky. As the company’s Managing Member, Walid Anas is responsible for business strategy, client relationships, and day-to-day operations.

With years of experience in digital marketing, Walid has helped businesses across industries amplify their brand presence, improve customer acquisition, and achieve sustainable growth. His vision was simple: to create a marketing agency that blends creativity with data, delivering not only bold ideas but also measurable results.

WALIDOS LLC is proud to operate as a U.S.-registered entity, providing transparent services and reliable partnerships. Clients trust us because we combine innovation with accountability, ensuring every campaign drives tangible results.
